Welcome to the Research Paper Toolbox

Constructing a research paper is an integral part of being a graduate student in the Information Architecture, Technical Communication, and Information Design programs at IIT.

There is no single “right” way to put a paper together, but many tools can make the process more efficient and manageable. In this course, you’ll find explanations of the basic steps involved in writing a research paper, as well as tools to complement each stage.

Whether you’re new to writing research papers or an experienced writer, this course will guide you through each step or allow you to refresh your skills. To begin, select a lesson by clicking on one of the modules below.
